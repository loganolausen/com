About Me ↓

Hey! I'm Logan Olausen, a multidisciplinary designer working as a remote freelancer.

Currently based down under in New Zealand, I have been in the graphic design industry for over 10 years in both NZ and abroad. I have collaborated with clients and agencies across the globe, which has given me the opportunity to work with some of the world’s leading brands, including UNESCO, Air New Zealand and Maxxis Tyres to name a few.

My multidisciplinary skillset spans across an array of print and digital applications with a particular focus in branding and visual identity. I love building brands from the ground up and being involved in the creative process every step along the way.
