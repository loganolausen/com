About Project ↓

Creating a slick and premium brand identity for an exclusive private membership club.

RIB Club is a private membership that offers exclusive access to its fleet of boats around the globe. Tasked with creating a new visual identity, I developed a premium and understated aesthetic to appeal to the brand's luxury lifestyle target market. This included logo design, brand guidelines, stationery, boat and office branding.
